# Amazon Prime Movies & TV Shows – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project performs an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Amazon Prime Movies and TV Shows to uncover insights related to content distribution, audience preferences, ratings, genres, and business impact.

The goal is to support data-driven decision-making for content strategy, engagement, and growth.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
- Analyze Amazon Prime content using EDA
- Identify trends in movies vs TV shows
- Understand genre, rating, runtime, and regional patterns
- Provide actionable business insights

---

## 📊 Dataset
The project uses two datasets:
- `titles.csv` – Details of movies and TV shows
- `credits.csv` – Cast and crew information

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed
- Data Cleaning & Wrangling
- Missing & Duplicate Value Handling
- Univariate & Bivariate Analysis
- 15 Data Visualizations
- Correlation & Pair Plot Analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Movies dominate the platform, but TV Shows have higher rating consistency
- Drama and Comedy are the most common genres
- Content quality matters more than runtime
- Popularity does not always equal high ratings
- Content is concentrated in recent years and few regions

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations
- Invest more in high-quality TV shows
- Improve promotion of high-rated but less popular content
- Diversify genres and production regions
- Expand family-friendly content
